chicken biriyani

Chicken biryani is a delicious and aromatic dish that's popular in Indian and South Asian cuisine. Here's a basic recipe to make chicken biryani:

Ingredients:

1 chicken 500g
2 basmati rice 2 cups
3 curd 1cup
4 ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon
5 red chili powder 1 teaspoon
6 ghee 1 tablespoons
Instructions:
  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well to coat the chicken pieces evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (preferably 2 hours in the refrigerator).
  2. Prepare the Rice:
    In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked rice, bay leaf, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and salt. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% done (parboiled). Drain the rice and set aside.
  3. Cook the chicken:
    Heat ghee or oil in a deep, heavy-bottomed pan or pressure cooker. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    Add the marinated chicken pieces along with chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is partially cooked and the tomatoes are softened.
  4. Layering the Biryani:
    • In the same pan (if large enough), or in a separate large pot, begin layering the biryani. Spread half of the partially cooked rice evenly over the chicken.
    • Sprinkle half of the chopped coriander leaves and mint leaves over the rice.
    • Repeat with the remaining rice, spreading it evenly
    • Sprinkle the remaining coriander leaves, mint leaves, and fried onions on top. Drizzle the saffron milk over the rice for added flavor and color.
  5. Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking):
    Cover the pan tightly with a lid. You can seal the edges with dough or a tight-fitting foil to trap the steam. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the biryani to cook in its own steam (dum) until the rice and chicken are fully cooked and infused with flavors.
